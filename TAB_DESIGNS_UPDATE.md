## Design System
- Primary accent: Cyan (cyan-500/cyan-600)
- Backgrounds: White with slate-50 for subtle variations
- Borders: Slate-200 (2px for emphasis)
- Text: Slate-800 for headers, slate-600 for labels, slate-500 for secondary
- Hover states: Slate-50 backgrounds, cyan for interactive elements
- Shadows: Subtle shadow-sm for depth
